24 Hour Sunset

A film by Édouard de Luze and Charles Derenne

24 Hour Sunset is a 2024 French-American documentary directed by Édouard de Luze and Charles Derenne. Shot entirely on 16mm film, the movie captures 24 hours in the creative life of Los Angeles through the eyes of iconic and emerging artists. It blends observational cinema, intimate interviews, and poetic imagery.

Featuring Kenneth Anger, Paul McCarthy, Raymond Pettibon, Louise Bonnet, Jill Mulleady, Henry Taylor, Ariana Papademetropoulos, Peter Shire, and Paz Lenchantin. Narrated by Joe Dallesandro.
