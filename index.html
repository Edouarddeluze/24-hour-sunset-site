24 Hour Sunset

A 16mm documentary film by Édouard de Luze & Charles Derenne

Description

24 Hour Sunset is a poetic, analog journey through the heart of the Los Angeles art scene. Shot entirely on 16mm film, the documentary follows 24 hours in the life of the city and its artists: Paul McCarthy, Raymond Pettibon, Jill Mulleady, Ariana Papademetropoulos, and others.
